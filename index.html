FAQ

Quick answers

Is it safe?

Yes. Everything runs inside your own browser — there are no servers, no accounts, and nothing about you or your boards ever leaves your computer.

Does it work on private and secret boards?

Yes. It works inside your logged-in Pinterest, so any board you can see, you can save.

Can I download videos?

Yes — videos are saved as ready-to-use MP4 files, in full quality.

Will it overwrite my files?

No. Re-downloading a board creates a fresh folder, and it skips pins you've already saved.

How many pins can it handle?

Whole boards and entire profiles. Large selections are split into several ZIPs automatically so your browser never runs out of memory.

Is downloading allowed?

Save content you have the right to use and respect creators' copyright. PinGrab is a tool for collecting your own references — how you use the files is up to you.
