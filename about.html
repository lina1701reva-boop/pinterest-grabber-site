Hi, I’m Alina 👋

I’m a digital artist and Procreate brush maker — and I basically live on Pinterest.

Every project I make starts the same way: hours on Pinterest, collecting references, color palettes, and ideas into boards. Pinterest is where my mood-boards live. (I also design and sell Procreate brushes over at revabrush.com.)

Why I built Revapin

The problem was always the same — getting those pins out of Pinterest. I wanted my boards as real files on my computer: to organize them, use them offline, drop them into a moodboard, or keep them before a pin disappears. But every tool I tried was clunky: paste this URL, log in here, wait for a server, get a blurry thumbnail.

So I built the thing I wished existed. Revapin lets you tick the exact pins you want right on the page and download them — as a ZIP, a tidy folder, or a PDF moodboard — in full quality, straight to your computer. Whole boards, every section, even a creator’s entire profile, in a click.

It’s made by an artist, for everyone who uses Pinterest to gather ideas — designers, illustrators, planners, marketers, and fellow mood-board addicts. Everything runs 100% in your browser: no account, no servers, and your boards stay yours.

The promise

Simple, fast, and private — that’s it. If something’s broken or you have an idea, I read every message: hello@revapin.com.
