Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

The short version: Revapin runs entirely inside your own browser. We have no servers that receive your data, no account to create, and no tracking. Files you download go straight from Pinterest to your computer — we never see them.

1. Who we are

Revapin (“Revapin,” “we,” “us”) is a Chrome browser extension that lets you select and download images and videos from Pinterest to your own device. Revapin is operated by Reva Creative LLC, a limited liability company registered in Sheridan, Wyoming, USA. Contact: lina1701reva@gmail.com.

2. Permissions we use, and why

  • Access to pinterest.com and pinimg.com — to display selection controls on pins and fetch the specific images or videos you choose to download. This happens entirely in your browser; we do not receive or store this data.
  • Downloads permission — to save your selected files to your browser’s Downloads folder.

We do not request access to any other websites and do not track activity outside Pinterest.

3. Data stored locally on your device

The extension stores your settings and usage state locally via chrome.storage (e.g., your selections and, on the Free plan, a monthly usage counter). This data stays on your device, is never transmitted to us, and is deleted when you remove the extension.

4. What we do not collect

We do not collect your name, boards, saved pins, browsing history, or analytics. The extension contains no trackers, analytics, or ad networks. We do not sell or share personal data.

5. Payments

All purchases are processed by Paddle (Paddle.com Market Limited), our authorized reseller and Merchant of Record, which handles your email and payment details under its own privacy policy. We never see full card details. We may receive confirmation of an active subscription and the associated email solely to manage your purchase and access.

6. Third parties

The only third parties involved are Pinterest and the payment provider above. No data is shared with anyone else.

7. Children’s privacy

Revapin is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. The “Last updated” date will reflect any changes.
